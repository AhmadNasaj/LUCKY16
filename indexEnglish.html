Lucky16 Game Rules

  • Weekly Matches
  • Schedule publication
  • Changing Predictions

Every week, 16 soccer matches will be featured. Each match will have three possible outcomes: "1" stands for a home team win, "2" stands for an away team win, and "X" stands for a tie. The home team is on the left in the tab, while the away team is on the right.

The match schedule will be published at least 4 days before the first match. All 16 results will be randomly assigned. During this time, users can change their predictions for each match up to 2 hours before the start of the first match.

It is the user's responsibility to access and change the results of the games according to their predictions. If the user does not make changes by the game's close, they will still participate in the contest with the randomly assigned results.


Note:
At each game wrap-up, interested users can download a file of all plays made by users.

Prizes and Categories

Win by guessing all 16 outcomes in the schedule. If no one guesses all the outcomes, the jackpot is accrued for the following week and added to the new week's bets until there is a winner.
Guess 15 out of 16 results in the schedule. If no one guesses 15 results, the prize of the second category goes to whoever guesses 14 out of 16 results. If there is no winner, even in this scenario, the winnings are awarded to whoever guesses 13 results. If there is still no winner even in this situation, the prize is awarded to the person who guesses 12 results If there is no winner, even with the 12 Points also, the jackpot of 15 will be accrued for the next week and added to the new week's bets until there is a winner.
If there is more than one winner in a category, the prize pool is divided equally among them. Also, if a user guesses all 16 results, they also participate in dividing the prize pool for those who guessed 15 results.

Lucky Weekly Contest:

Every week, besides the prize pools for the two categories, there is an additional exclusive prize pool for Lucky, Lucky1, Lucky2 and Lucky 3 users. This prize pool will be awarded to the user who totals the most points by adding up the results of the 3 ballots played.

Important Notes:
If a match is canceled or postponed for more than 24 hours, its result will be counted for everyone, except for more than 4 canceled or postponed matches, in which prize money will be accrued for the following week. The exception is the Lucky Weekly Race, whose prize pool will always be awarded. All prizes and rankings can be accessed from the app and will be updated in real time, and prize totals can also be seen on the blockchain address you will always find on the website.

Membership Plans

Ladybug

By purchasing the Silver package, the user is granted the right to participate in 4 weeks of the game. Each week, the user is entitled to make a bet to guess the results of the matches.

Owl

By purchasing the Gold package, the user is granted the right to participate in 4 weeks of play. Each week, the user is entitled to make two bets to guess the results of the matches

Lucky

With the purchase of the Platinum package, the user is granted the right to participate in 4 weeks of play. Each week, the user is entitled to make three bets to guess the results of the matches.

This prize pool is awarded to the user who gets the most points by adding up the results of the three weekly plays.

Remember that purchasing one of the packages above allows you to participate in the scheduled game weeks and place the required bets to try to win the jackpots. Each package offers a different number of plays, so you can choose according to your preferences and the level of participation you wish to have in the game.
